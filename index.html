Biography

I’m a PhD student of inorganic chemistry at Empa/Uni Basel, where I research copper based dye densitized solar cells (DSSCs).

My lifelong fascination with the natural sciences, both in experimentation and theory, motivated me to major in physical chemistry, where I could combine my interests in experimental investigation with my passion for mathematical modelling.

Outside of the natural sciences, I’m also interested in electronics and computer programming.
